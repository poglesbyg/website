August 25, 2016

What exactly is hypertext? This notion of an original author is theoretically not possible. Different types of text. Constructing meaning of text. Readerly versus writerly text. Make the reader no longer a consumer, but an active particpant. Matthew Kireshembaum writes about afternoon. How do we define the meaning of a text? How do we define the actual text? Colors. Font. There is never a fixed version of a text. Depending upon who reads it, but also when and where it is read. Cognitive make-up. Reader-response critism.

"to write a novel that changes each time you read it" This novel has instructions that it follows. Pages and decisions are specific reactions and defaults.
